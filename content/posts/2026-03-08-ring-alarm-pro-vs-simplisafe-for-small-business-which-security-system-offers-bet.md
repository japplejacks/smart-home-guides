# Ring Alarm Pro vs SimpliSafe for Small Business: Which Security System Offers Better Remote Monitoring and Cost Savings?

Choosing the right security system for your small business is crucial for protecting your assets, employees, and peace of mind. Two popular options that frequently come up in discussions are Ring Alarm Pro and SimpliSafe. Both systems offer compelling features for business owners, but which one delivers better remote monitoring capabilities and cost savings?

As someone who's helped numerous Atlanta-area businesses implement smart security solutions, I've seen firsthand how the right system can make or break a company's security strategy. Let's dive deep into these two platforms to help you make an informed decision.

## Understanding Your Small Business Security Needs

Before comparing specific systems, it's essential to understand what small businesses typically need from a security solution:

- **24/7 remote monitoring capabilities**
- **Cost-effective monthly fees**
- **Easy installation and maintenance**
- **Scalability as the business grows**
- **Integration with existing business tools**
- **Professional monitoring services**
- **Mobile access for on-the-go management**

Both Ring Alarm Pro and SimpliSafe address these needs, but they take different approaches to delivering value.

## Ring Alarm Pro: The Connected Business Solution

### Key Features and Benefits

The [Ring Alarm Pro](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Ring+Alarm+Pro&tag=applecore03-20) stands out as more than just a security system—it's a comprehensive smart business hub. Here's what makes it compelling for small businesses:

**Built-in Wi-Fi 6 Router**: Unlike traditional security systems, Ring Alarm Pro includes a powerful Wi-Fi 6 router, potentially eliminating the need for separate networking equipment. This dual functionality can reduce overall IT costs for small businesses.

**Cellular Backup**: The system includes built-in cellular connectivity, ensuring your security monitoring continues even if your internet goes down—a critical feature for business continuity.

**Professional Monitoring**: Ring's professional monitoring service costs $20 per month and includes 24/7 monitoring, cellular backup, and unlimited video recording for Ring cameras.

### Remote Monitoring Capabilities

Ring Alarm Pro excels in remote monitoring through:

- **Real-time notifications** sent directly to your smartphone
- **Live video streaming** from connected Ring cameras
- **Two-way audio communication** with visitors or potential intruders
- **Geofencing capabilities** for automatic arming/disarming
- **Integration with Alexa** for voice control and status updates

The Ring app provides a comprehensive dashboard where business owners can monitor multiple locations, review event history, and manage user access permissions—all from anywhere with internet connectivity.

### Cost Analysis for Small Businesses

**Initial Investment**:
- Ring Alarm Pro base station: ~$270
- Additional sensors: $20-40 each
- Professional installation (optional): $100-200

**Monthly Costs**:
- Professional monitoring: $20/month
- No long-term contracts required

**Hidden Value**: The included Wi-Fi 6 router could save businesses $100-300 annually on networking equipment and internet service provider router rental fees.

## SimpliSafe: The Business-Focused Security Specialist

### Key Features and Benefits

SimpleSafe has built its reputation on providing straightforward, reliable security solutions. For small businesses, it offers:

**Professional-Grade Equipment**: SimpliSafe's sensors and base station are designed for commercial use, with longer battery life and more robust construction than many consumer-grade alternatives.

**Flexible Monitoring Options**: Unlike Ring, SimpliSafe offers multiple monitoring tiers, allowing businesses to choose the level of service that matches their budget and needs.

**No-Contract Options**: While contracts can provide cost savings, SimpliSafe also offers month-to-month service for businesses that need flexibility.

### Remote Monitoring Capabilities

SimpleSafe's remote monitoring includes:

- **24/7 professional monitoring** with trained security specialists
- **Mobile app control** for arming, disarming, and status checking
- **Smart alerts** that distinguish between different types of events
- **Environmental monitoring** for smoke, water, and temperature changes
- **Integration capabilities** with other business systems

The SimpliSafe mobile app is particularly well-regarded for its intuitive interface and reliable performance, making it easy for business owners to stay connected to their security system.

### Cost Analysis for Small Businesses

**Initial Investment**:
- Basic business system: $200-400
- Additional sensors: $15-50 each
- Professional installation: $79-199

**Monthly Costs**:
- Self-monitoring: $0
- Professional monitoring: $17.99-27.99/month
- 3-year contract discounts available

## The Verdict: Which System Wins?

For pure security functionality and cost-effectiveness, SimpliSafe edges ahead with its specialized focus and flexible pricing options. However, Ring Alarm Pro offers compelling value when you factor in the included networking capabilities and smart ecosystem integration.

**Best Overall Value**: Ring Alarm Pro wins for businesses that need networking upgrades or want extensive smart features.

**Best Pure Security Solution**: SimpliSafe takes the crown for businesses focused solely on security monitoring.

**Best for Budget-Conscious Businesses**: SimpliSafe offers more predictable costs and lower entry barriers.
